Logo

Mark & Usage

The ApartmentIQ logo is a monochrome grid/building icon + wordmark. They are a single unit — never separate them. Use SVG for screen, @3x PNG for PDF pipelines.

Rules

Default position: upper-left corner. Maintain clear space equal to the icon height on all sides. Never stretch, skew, recolor, or add effects. Minimum height: 12px. Aspect ratio is fixed at ~5.93:1.

Product Sub-Brands

Product names (Market Surveys, Explore Pro, Explore Pro+, Daylight) are displayed in their respective gradients at large scale on section dividers. Daylight additionally uses "By ApartmentIQ" beneath the wordmark.

Voice & Tone

How We Talk

Authoritative and grounded. We are the data company. Direct, short sentences — lead with the point. Technically precise but never academic. Modern without being trendy.

Use these words

  • automated, daily, real-time
  • transparent, modern, public data
  • proprietary, explainable
  • all-in-one, deep/deeply
  • pioneered

Avoid these words

  • revolutionary, game-changing
  • cutting-edge, leverage (as verb)
  • best-in-class, world-class
  • empower, seamless, robust
  • end-to-end, solution (in isolation)

Writing Style

Paragraphs are 1–2 sentences max. Fragments for emphasis: "Built for this." "The end of guesswork." Pair short declarative statements with longer explanatory follow-ups.

No exclamation marks in headlines. Sentence case, not title case or ALL CAPS. Full-sentence headlines end with a period; fragment headlines get no punctuation. Oxford comma: yes.

Headlines

Headline Patterns

Short, declarative. One accent word in gradient per headline max. Under ~8 words when possible. Never accent more than two consecutive words.

Built for this.
Big Decisions. Bad data.
The end of guesswork.
Fully automated. Deeply detailed.

Daylight's "by design" Pattern

Daylight uses a unique structure: [Gradient adjective] + "by design" as a two-line headline. Examples: "Transparent by design," "Precise by design," "Strategic by design." The gradient word uses Daylight's warm gradient (pink → orange). "by design" is always regular weight in text-primary.

Key Rhythm Rules

Headline → Subhead: 24px (tight — they're a visual unit). Subhead → Body: 32px. Body → Next block: 48px. Section → Section: 64px minimum. Page edges: 64px margins on all sides.

Max 3 content blocks per slide. No text block longer than 3 lines without a visual break. Target 40–50% whitespace.
